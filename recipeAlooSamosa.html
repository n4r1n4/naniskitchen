Aloo Samosa
TIME: 45 minutes SERVINGS: 15 SPECIAL EQUIPMENT: cooking pan, deep fry pan
Directions
Instructions
  1. Heat oil in a cooking pan. Add in onions, ginger garlic paste, and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add in the spice powders and mix well. Then, add in grated carrots and vegetables and cook them.
  3. Sprinkle in salt, tomato ketchup, and mix well. Add in boiled potatoes and combine. Finally, add in coriander leaves and mix.
  4. Mix flour with water to make a thick paste (this is used for folding the samosa). Set this aside until needed.
  5. Take a samosa sheet, spoon in the filling. Fold it like a samosa.
  6. Repeat this with the remaining samosas (you can store them in a container in the freezer until needed).
  7. Heat oil for deep frying. Drop the samosas in and fry on medium-high heat till golden brown and crispy.
  8. Strain using a slotted spoon and serve with ketchup.
