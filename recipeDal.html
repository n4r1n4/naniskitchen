Dal Makhani
TIME: 45 minutes SERVINGS: 5 SPECIAL EQUIPMENT: cooking pan
Directions
  • ½ cup whole urad dal
  • 3 ½ cups water
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp masala powder
  • 1 cup onions
  • ½ tsp cumin seed
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 tomato
  • 3 tbsp cilantro
  • 1 tsp salt
Instructions
  1. Combine the lentils, water, whole garlic cloves, and turmeric.
  2. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat, and cover.
  3. Cook until the lentils have disintegrated, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Heat a small skillet over medium-low heat and add the oil.
  6. When the oil starts to shimmer, add the onions and sweat them until soft and translucent.
  7. Toss in the diced jalapeno and cook for another minute.
  8. Add the cumin seeds.
  9. Toss in the tomato and cilantro and cook until the tomato is just warmed through.
  10. Fold the mixture into the dal and add the salt.
  11. Ready to serve as a main dish with an Indian flatbread.
