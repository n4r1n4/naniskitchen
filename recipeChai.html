Masala Chai
TIME: 20 minutes SERVINGS: 5 SPECIAL EQUIPMENT: cooking pot, mortar and pestle, mesh strainer
Directions
  • 1 whole cinnamon stick
  • 6-8 green cardamom pods
  • 3-4 whole cloves
  • 2 Â½ cups water
  • 2 Tbsp grated ginger
  • 3 Tbsp loose leaf black tea
  • 2 cups dairy-free milk
  • 4 Tbsp sugar
Instructions
  1. Add cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves to the bowl of a mortar and use a pestle to slightly crush the spices (not into a powder, just small pieces).
  2. To a pot, add crushed spices, water, and grated ginger. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Reduce heat slightly to medium-low and maintain a simmer for 15 minutes or until it reduces by about one-third.
  4. Add tea and dairy-free milk of choice, lower heat to low, cover, and continue cooking for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Turn off heat and let steep for 5 minutes more (covered) or longer for a deeper flavor.
